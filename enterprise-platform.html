01 — The Platform

What this platform does

"Specialty insurance is one of the most complex, high-stakes domains in enterprise software. Every decision an underwriter makes can be worth millions."

This is an AI-native Specialty Insurance Intelligence Platform built for the complexity of specialty lines — Aviation, Marine, Construction, and Political Violence & Terrorism.

The platform solves a fundamental problem: underwriters were spending 40% of their day on non-value-added activity — chasing documents, rekeying data, juggling spreadsheets, and making decisions without portfolio-level context.

The platform changes that. Submissions are ingested automatically. Risk signals are surfaced instantly. Portfolio exposure is visible at every decision point. Underwriters write better business, faster, with the confidence to back every bind.

The Domain

Specialty insurance — Aviation, Marine, Construction, Political Violence & Terrorism. Not commodity insurance. Complex, high-value, regulated risk.

The Problem

40% of an underwriter's day lost to non-value-added work — manual systems, fragmented data, zero portfolio visibility.

The Shift

From days to minutes. From fragmented tools to one intelligent platform. From gut feel to data-backed decisions.

02 — My Role

What I do here

"I joined as the sole designer. I am now the most senior design voice across the entire platform."

Three years ago I was handed a single product and no design system. Today I lead a team of 3, own design decisions across 5 lines of business, and work directly with the Product Director on all major architectural decisions. My title is Staff UX Designer. My actual role is closer to design strategist, systems architect, and team builder combined.

End-to-end product ownership

Pre-bind submission intake through post-bind workbench — across Marine, Aviation, Construction, and PVT. I own both sides of the underwriter's entire day.

Direct product leadership collaboration

All major design decisions, new feature directions, and process changes are made in direct collaboration with the Product Director and senior product management. Not executing briefs — shaping strategy.

Built and led the design team

Started solo. Recruited, onboarded, and now lead a team of 3 designers. Responsible for all team processes, ways of working, and quality standards across every LOB.

Unified design system architecture

Led the complete redesign of a 10-year-old design system during company rebrand. Built a unified component library scalable across all LOBs and multiple clients — reducing design and dev time, eliminating inconsistency, and enabling modular feature additions without redesign.

Horizontal user journey design

Designed a single horizontal workflow architecture that works across any LOB and any client — with necessary customisations layered on top. One system. Infinite scale. Each modular feature addition can be independently priced — directly enabling company revenue growth.

AI-driven design process

Introduced AI into the team's design workflow to eliminate repetitive tasks, reduce rework, and increase output quality. Reduced project timelines from 6 weeks to 2–3 weeks.

Process and team decision authority

Responsible for all design team process decisions — how we work, what tools we use, how we collaborate with product and engineering. Every significant change to team process goes through me.
